FAQ's

What time do GreenVine deliver?


Our drivers set off early on carbon-efficient routes. So, we can't offer delivery time slots. But you can choose to receive on-the-day delivery notifications.

Where do GreenVine deliver to?


We visit each area on the same day every week. Use our postcode checker to see if we can deliver to you.

Where is GreenVine based?


We're based in Bath. But work with amazing makers across the UK. When we do source from overseas, we never use air freight.
