About us

Since 1988, we've believed that food has the power to help change the world. We're on a mission to make shopping sustainably simple, putting people and our planet first every step of the way.

Our Story & Mission

Welcome to GreenVine Market, your local organic grocery store nestled in the heart of Bath. Our journey began with a simple yet profound vision: to provide our community with access to the freshest, most wholesome organic produce and homemade goods, all while championing sustainable and eco-friendly practices.

We believe in connecting people with the source of their food, fostering a healthier lifestyle, and supporting a greener planet. Every item in our store is carefully selected, ensuring it meets our rigorous standards for quality, taste, and ethical sourcing.

Homemade Goods

Our Commitment to Sustainability

At GreenVine Market, sustainability isn't just a buzzword, it's at the core of everything we do. We meticulously source our products from local, certified organic farms that share our commitment to environmental stewardship and ethical practices.

  • Local Partnerships: We prioritize partnerships with nearby farmers to reduce our carbon footprint and support the local economy.
  • Eco-Friendly Packaging: We use recyclable packaging wherever possible, encouraging customers to bring their own bags.
  • Waste Reduction: We implement strategies to minimize food waste, including composting and donating surplus produce.
  • Fair Trade: We ensure fair compensation for our farmers and producers, promoting equitable throughout our supply chain.

By choosing GreenVine Market, you're not just buying groceries, you're supporting a movement towards a more sustainable and healthy future.

Weekly Organic Produce Boxes

Simplify your healthy eating with our convenient weekly organic produce boxes! Each box is thoughtfully curated with a seasonal selection of the freshest fruits and vegetables, delivered right to your door in Bath and surrounding areas.

Enjoy variety, discover new ingredients, and rest assured that your produce is always organic and sustainably sourced. Plus, with Fruit & Veg Box Swaps, you can customize your box to ensure you always get what you love.
